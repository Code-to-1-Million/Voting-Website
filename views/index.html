Welcome to LSMS PTSA

The LSMS PTSA is dedicated to supporting the students, teachers, and families of LSMS. Our goal is to promote a positive and enriching educational experience for all members of our community.

About LSMS PTSA

The LSMS PTSA is a volunteer-run organization comprised of parents, teachers, and staff who are dedicated to supporting the students of LSMS. Our mission is to promote a positive and enriching educational experience for all members of our community through various programs, events, and initiatives.

Contact LSMS PTSA

If you have any questions or would like to get involved with LSMS PTSA, please don't hesitate to reach out. You can contact us by email at ptsa@lsms.edu or by phone at (555) 555-5555.
